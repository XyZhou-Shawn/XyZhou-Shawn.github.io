About Me

I am currently a 3rd year Ph.D. candidate at Wangxuan Institute of Computer Technology, Peking University, supervised by Prof. Yongtao Wang. My research focuses on 3D vision, spatial intelligence, and world model, with an emphasis on learning expressive 3D/4D representations for scene reconstruction, controllable generation, simulation, and open-ended understanding. I am particularly interested in geometry-grounded generative and reconstructive models that bridge visual synthesis and the physical world, aiming to build realistic, interactive, and self-evolving 4D intelligent systems.
